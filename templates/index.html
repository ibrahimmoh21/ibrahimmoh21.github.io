Message from the Founder and CEO

I would like to start by thanking you for taking the time to read this message and for getting a better understanding of my company Shanoor Trading Co LLC (STC). To begin with, I will share the origins of the name of the company. The story behind our name reflects our vision—'Sha' derived from 'Shahi' meaning royal, and 'Noor' meaning light. We aspire to be a shining light, leading the way in our industry.

Established in 2004, the concept of business was to meet a critical gap in the UAE construction/maintenance industry at that time. Providing value for money, high quality and globally sourced products while ensuring on-time, in-full (OTIF) deliveries. More than 20 years later, while our core business model remains unchanged, we have continuously enhanced our competitive pricing and broadened our product offerings.

As we look to the future, our next chapter will focus on forging lasting partnerships and addressing the ever-growing needs of the UAE market.
