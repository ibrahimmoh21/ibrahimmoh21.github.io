Company Profile

Established in 2004, Shanoor Trading Co LLC has been a leading building material supplier in the UAE, providing high-quality products for over two decades. We offer a comprehensive range of materials, including Power tools, MEP components, Safety material, General Hardware, Consumables and much more. Our commitment to quality ensures that our materials meet the highest standards for durability, safety, and sustainability and our nothing short of Best in Class grades.

At Shanoor Trading Co LLC, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We work closely with our customers and partners to provide tailored solutions that meet the specific needs of each project, ensuring timely delivery and competitive pricing. Our experienced team and robust logistics network allow us to support projects of all sizes, from small renovations to large-scale developments.

Over the years, we have contributed to numerous iconic projects across the UAE, earning a reputation for reliability, excellence, and service. Our unique strength lies in offering a diverse range of high-quality, globally sourced building materials, coupled with exceptional customer service and timely delivery, making Shanoor Trading Co LLC a trusted partner for projects of all sizes.
